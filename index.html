Greenleaf Store Perfomance

The GreenLeaf Stores Business Performance Report (May 2025) reviews the company’s first-year growth as a sustainable home products retailer. It highlights strong profitability with a 70% margin and top performance from lighting products, though sales fluctuated mid-year and returns were moderate. Ohio led in revenue while California lagged. The report recommends focusing on high-margin items, improving logistics, and adopting data-driven strategies to sustain growth.

Problem Mapping:Fish Bone Diagram

In this project, I created a fishbone diagram for Barrel & Brew to uncover the root causes of their operational challenges. I analyzed issues across People, Processes, Equipment, Materials, Environment, and Measurement, and discovered key problems like overworked staff, aging equipment, and poor data tracking. This visualization helped highlight where inefficiencies were coming from and guided clear actions for improving operations and overall performance.

Unilever Canteen Process Mapping
